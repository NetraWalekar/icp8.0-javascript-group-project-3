How to order the Product?
1.Browse Products:- Navigate through our website to explore a wide range of products available in different categories.
2.Add to Cart:- Once you find an item you wish to purchase, select the desired size, color (if applicable), and quantity, then click "Add to Cart".
3.Review Shopping Cart:- Click on the shopping cart to review the items added. Here, you can update quantities or remove items if needed.
4.Proceed to Checkout:- After reviewing your cart, click "Proceed to Checkout" to begin the checkout process.
5.Sign In or Checkout as Guest:- If you have an existing account, sign in using your email address and password. New customers can choose to checkout as a guest or create a new account for added benefits such as order tracking and faster checkout in the future.
6.Enter Shipping Information:- Provide the shipping address where you would like your order to be delivered. Ensure all details are accurate to avoid delays.
7.Select Shipping Method:- Choose from the available shipping options (e.g., standard, expedited) based on your preference and delivery timeframe.
8.Enter Payment Information:- Securely enter your payment details, including credit/debit card information or select an alternative payment method like PayPal.
9.Apply Promotions or Discounts (if applicable):- Enter any promotional codes or discounts you have during the checkout process to apply savings to your order.
10.Review and Place Order:- Review your order summary, including items, shipping details, and total cost. Confirm that everything is correct before clicking "Place Order".
11.Order Confirmation:- Once your order is successfully placed, you will receive an order confirmation email with details of your purchase and an order number for reference.
12.Track Your Order:- Monitor the status of your order by logging into your account or using the tracking information provided in your confirmation email.
What is delivery process?
Once order is placed online, after confirmation, it is pushed to the nearest store of your delivery address through an automated process. The order is picked and packed, checked for quality, physical condition, etc. by a dedicated team of trained staff and then sent for delivery at your door step. The order will be delivered in the chosen timeslot by you. Once the order leaves the store for delivery, a notification will be sent on your registered email address and mobile number. In case of any changes in the delivery schedule or ordered quantity, you will be notified well beforehand.
Can I change my order once it has been placed?
Yes, you can still modify your order once it has been placed. However, once the order has been dispatched from the store upon which a delivery notification will be sent on your registered email address and contact number, no further changes can be made in the order. In case you wish to remove certain products from the order, there always is an option of returning the products. In case you wish to order some additional products, you can always proceed placing a new order.
What if I didn't like the quality of the products delivered to me?
We would request you to check the products while accepting the products during delivery. In case you are not satisfied with the quality of the products, please return those products to our Delivery Expert. Our Delivery Expert will enter details of the same in the mobile tracking app to initiate request for return as well as refund in case of any. In case of Postpaid order, you will be required to pay the adjusted amount only for the products accepted by you. In case of Prepaid orders, request for refund will be initiated and cash will be credited into your Natures Basket online account, which can be redeemed while placing orders the next time.
