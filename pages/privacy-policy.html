Privacy Policy

1. Definitions

Cookies: A small file placed on your device by our website or mobile application when you visit or use certain features.

Data: Personal information and SPDI about you, which could allow you to be identified when you visit our stores, website, and/or mobile application.

Data Protection Laws: Any applicable law relating to the processing of Data.

Partners: Select third parties with whom we have contracts to offer products or services to you or our other users.

Service Providers: Entities to whom we disclose your Data for processing to provide services to us.

SPDI: Sensitive personal data or information as defined by the Data Protection Laws.

IRCPL: A company incorporated in India.

Tata Group Entity: Tata Sons Private Limited and its subsidiaries, affiliates, associate companies, and joint venture companies.

User or you: The natural person who accesses our stores, website, or mobile application.

2. What Data Do We Collect About You

  • Contact information: name, email address, postal address, country, employer, phone number.
  • Financial information: payment instrument information, transactions, transaction history, preferences.
  • Technical information: website, device and mobile app usage, IP address, cookies, pixels.
  • Transaction information: date of transaction, total amount, transaction history, preferences.
  • Product and service information: account membership number, registration and payment information.
  • Personal information: age, sex, date of birth, marital status, nationality, government identification details.
  • Reviews, feedback, and opinions about our products and services.
  • Loyalty programme information: membership information, account details, profile or password details.
  • Payment instrument information: managed by third-party service providers.

3. How We Collect Data

  • Information You Give Us: Any information you enter on our website or mobile application or give us in any other way.
  • Automatic Information We Collect: Cookies, pixels, and similar technologies.
  • E-mail Communications: Confirmation when you open an email from us or click on a link in the email.
  • Information from Other Sources: Data from third-party websites you authorize to interact with our website or mobile application.
  • Information Previously Provided to Tata Group Entities: Shared information with your consent.

4. Data Shared by You

  • When you register with us to receive our products and/or services.
  • When you conduct a transaction with us or attempt a transaction.
  • When you complete surveys conducted by or for us.
  • When you elect to receive any communications (including promotional offers) from us.
  • From the information gathered by your visit to our stores, website or mobile application.
  • By way of correlation or inferences from information sourced from other sources based on your consent.

5. Data that is Collected Automatically

  • IP address, operating system details, browsing details, device details, language settings.
  • Aggregated information to measure the number of visits, average time spent on the site, pages viewed.
  • Cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to collect data in line with settings on your browser.

6. Our Use of Data

  • To carry out our obligations arising from any contract entered into between you and us.
  • To provide products and/or services and communicate with you about products and/or services.
  • To enable payment, billing, and invoicing related purposes.
  • To provide you with offers, personalized services, and recommendations.
  • To operate, evaluate, and improve our business, website, and mobile application.
  • To communicate with you and respond to your requests, questions, feedback, claims, or disputes.
  • To protect against and prevent fraud, illegal activity, harm, financial loss, and other legal or information security risks.
  • To serve other purposes as authorized or required by applicable law.

7. Minors

Our website and mobile application do not offer products or services for use by minors. If you are under 18, you may use our website or mobile application only with the consent of a parent or your guardian.

8. Sharing of Data

  • Partners: Services, products, or applications provided by Partners.
  • Tata Group Entities: To offer products, services, and benefits.
  • Service Providers: For storing and analyzing Data, protecting our systems, providing customer service, processing payments.
  • Protecting IRCPL: To comply with applicable law or legal process, enforce our agreements, protect against harm or financial loss.

9. Keeping Data Secure

We use technical and organizational measures to safeguard your Data and store it on secure servers. If you suspect any misuse or loss or unauthorized access to your Data, please let us know immediately by contacting us by e-mail.

10. Retention of Data

We retain Data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, to resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, comply with legal obligations, or as long as necessary to provide products and services. Data may persist in copies made for backup and business continuity purposes for additional time.

11. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on applicable Data Protection Laws, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your Data, withdraw your consent, or lodge a complaint with the relevant regulatory authority. You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us by following the instructions in those communications.
